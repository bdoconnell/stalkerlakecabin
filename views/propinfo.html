Our cabin is located on Stalker Lake in Otter Tail County. It's a 2 to 3 hour drive from the Twin Cities, just off of Highway 94. The cabin sits on a 7 acre wooded parcel that separates our cabin from the other cabins and provides the privacy you're looking for when you're "at the lake". You can enjoy an evening around the fire or grilling on the deck without neighbors 30 feet to either side. For nature watchers we have 500 feet of undisturbed lakefront that attracts songbirds, humming birds and butterflies. We also have mowed trails for a walk in the woods to see if the deer and turkeys might be feeding in the food plots. As the pictures suggest, the fishing is very good. Stalker Lake is 1300 acres and depending on the time of year, you can catch Sunfish, Largemouth and Northern Pike right from the dock. You can also jig or troll for Walleyes that are usually 100 yards out from our dock along the drop-off. And for the Golfer's, Stalker Lake Golf Course is less than 1 mile away.
